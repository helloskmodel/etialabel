The Next Generation of Durable Labels
Flexcon® NexGen™ label solutions offer reliable performance in industrial durable labeling applications.
These pressure-sensitive
durable labeling solutions
include polyesters, polypropylenes, and vinyls and are made to meet many different types of application needs.

Cross-platform printability
This NexGen™ line features Flexcon’s two most dependable topcoats – matte MTC-722 and gloss TC-716 – which are compatible with various print platforms including thermal transfer, narrow-format digital, and conventional methods.
Reliable Adhesion
Products are paired with three adhesive systems that bond to the most challenging surfaces: L-344, L-606, and/or L-778.
UL/cUL and CSA ready portfolios
Save time and money with pre-approved products. Flexcon has the most extensive
UL-recognized products
in the industry as well as the UL expertise to guide you.
Durable industrial labels to keep your production moving
Flexcon® NexGen™ label solutions work on standard metals and plastics, as well as tough oily and low surface energy surfaces. Heavier coat weights are also available for rough and textured surfaces.
Our label materials are engineered for real-world performance to adapt to the demands of high-speed production lines.
