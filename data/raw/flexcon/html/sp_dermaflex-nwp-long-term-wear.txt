Wearable medical device adhesive with 30+ day wear time on intact skin
Flexcon® DermaFlex™ NWP (non-woven polyester) with H-30 biocompatible skin contact adhesive.
Request information
Download Brochure
Long-term wear medical device adhesive medical overtape
DermaFlex™ NWP 240 Flesh with H-30 skin-friendly adhesive
is intended to secure glucose monitors, insulin pumps, ostomy bags, and other long-wear skin-adhered devices on intact skin.
This new adhesive, H-30, can confidently be used in applications that require adhesion to the skin for 30+ days.
Read the
DermaFlex™ 30+ Day Wear Study.
Conformable and Comfortable
The 2.4 mil non-woven polyester (NWP) is built to move with the user—flexing and stretching to match every motion. Its neutral flesh tone blends well across a wide range of skin tones, while the breathable design lets moisture escape to help keep skin dry. Comfortable and reliable, it supports extended wear without compromising performance or patient comfort.
Reliable hold that strengthens over time
This NWP overtape with H-30 biocompatible high-tack adhesive strengthens over time to deliver a lasting, dependable hold. It's shower safe and made for long-term wear, giving users confidence through daily activity. Designed to adhere to low-surface energy (LSE) materials on wearable devices, it provides strong performance for securely bonding wearable devices to intact skin.
Biocompatibility
DermaFlex™ medical adhesives have been tested for biocompatibility (skin irritation, sensitization, and cytotoxicity) by Flexcon’s adhesive suppliers using ISO-10993 or similar test methods. DermaFlex™ skin-friendly materials are intended for device attachment to the body.

DermaFlex™ H-30 Biocompatibility Statement.
Consistent quality
Our change management process is designed to ensure consistent material performance. No raw component substitutions without a six-month change notification.
