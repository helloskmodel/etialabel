Adhesive Transfer Tape Uses
Flexcon Adhesive Transfer Tape is used for bonding and mounting in indoor and outdoor applications such as auto interiors; electronics; and appliances. Adhesive transfer tapes solve bonding issues on hard-to-stick and textured surfaces like powder-coated metals and LSE plastics, with strong adhesives that resist heat, shear, and cold flow.
Engineered to bond rigid, textured, or flexible substrates, our adhesive transfer tape ensures durable, high-strength attachment across diverse industrial and automotive surfaces—making it ideal for sensors, trims, graphic overlays, and electronic assemblies.
Converter-Friendly Format
Offered on double-faced release liners and compatible with roll-to-sheet or roll-form converting, our tapes simplify automation and die-cut processing, maximizing throughput for high-volume operations.
