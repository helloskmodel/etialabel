Download brochure
Wearable medical device adhesives
Flexcon® DermaFlex™ NNRU  skin-friendly wearable device adhesive materials are intended for device attachment to the body such as glucose monitors, insulin pumps, or pulse oximeters.
DermaFlex™ NNRU products can confidently be used for applications that require adhesion to the skin for an extended period of time. Read the
DermaFlex™  Wear Study
.
Comfortable for Patients
The challenge? Finding a substrate that's thin enough to be lightweight, flexible enough to move with the body without pulling, and permeable enough to allow the skin to breathe - all while staying adhered for the prescribed period of time without causing irritation.
Benefits
DermaFlex™ NNRU
disappears on the body in both sight and feel.
It's thin and flexible enough to conform to body counters and
stretch with movement
, but also return to its original dimensions.
The adhesive has been tested for biocompatibility by Flexcon's adhesive suppliers using ISO-10993 or similar test methods.
The adhesive provides excellent
long-term adhesion to human skin
.
Rigid Enough for Converting
The challenge for converters is that the films that meet comfort requirements are flimsy, making them very difficult to process because they tend to fold back on themselves like plastic wrap, even when coated with adhesive.
DermaFlex™ NNRU is a .001” clear nylon reinforced polyurethane film.
It’s much easier to convert than your typical 1 mil PU film because the nylon reinforcement provides enhanced dimensional stability.
It’s compatible with EtO and gamma sterilization procedures.
The 50 lb. kraft release liner offers a good diecutting base.
Customizable
We can change the DermaFlex™ adhesive, adjust the adhesive coat weight, or swap out the liner to meet the unique specifications of your application.
Call (508) 885-8440 for more information or to order samples.
More Than Medical
This technology isn’t being limited to medical. Wearable devices have the potential to replace personal tracking devices worn as bracelets or necklaces by allowing consumers to wear their devices directly on the skin and out of sight. DermaFlex™ NNRU is rigid enough to convert easily and flexible enough to provide optimum comfort.
