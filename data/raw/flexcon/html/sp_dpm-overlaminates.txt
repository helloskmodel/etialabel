Protect durable labels with Flexcon overlaminates
Enhance and protect printed labels for the consumer durables and transportation markets with overlaminates.
Request information
Overlaminates for maximum label protection and excellent image quality
Flexcon overlaminates provide protection against environmental factors such as UV rays, moisture, and abrasion to ensure your graphics maintain their integrity resulting in extended lifespan and reducing maintenance costs.

choosing the best overlaminate for your application.

Outdoor durability
Flexcon overlaminates are engineered to safeguard inks and the base substrate. They ensure long-lasting durability, while offering reliable protection for diverse indoor applications and provide outstanding 7-year outdoor anti-graffiti protection, maintaining surface integrity in challenging environments.
Gloss and matte finish
Flexcon offers a variety of overlaminate finishes, including clear, gloss, velvet embossed, and matte, to enhance and protect your labels with aestheic appeal and durability.
Printability
Flexcon's durable overlaminates protect printed graphics from UV rays, moisture, scratches, temperature extremes, abrasion, and chemicals. Select products are compatible with various printing techniques, including flexo, screen, letterpress, offset, and thermal transfer, making them ideal for durable signage, labels, and decals.
Abrasion resistant
