High-Performance Mounting Adhesives
Durable mounting adhesives can be the perfect alternative to mechanical fasteners. Certain applications may require a simple transfer adhesive, while more complex applications, where dissimilar surfaces warrant different adhesives, will require a double-sided product to adhere the two surfaces together. High-performance adhesives are available in a variety of configurations, including foams, tapes and single- or double-sided mounting films.
