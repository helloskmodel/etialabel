Technical Data and Details
Availability
Benefits
Adhesive exhibits exceptional performance on textured and uneven surfaces
UL recognized under UL 969 - UL File No. PGGU2.MH10170 Marking and Labeling System Materials - Component
Click to view PDF
Contact: +1 508 885 8200
Features
Permanent acrylic adhesive offers medium tack, high shear and excellent adhesion to a variety of metal and plastic surfaces, including polypropylene, polyethylene, PORON® and Kushon® cellular rubber products
Adhesive offers high initial tack, high shear, and high ultimate bond to a wide variety of rough textured surfaces, including low-surface energy plastics and painted metal
Backed with an 84 lb. natural kraft polycoated layflat differential release liner
Heavy release liner has excellent moisture stability and is a good base for flatbed and steel rule diecutting
