Features
1.0 clear matte polyester has excellent abrasion and humidity resistance and excellent clarity and label protection
Film protects the underlying graphics from harsh environmental conditions
Permanent acrylic adhesive offers high shear and excellent adhesion to metals, and high-surface energy plastics, painted metal, powder-coated paint, polycarbonate and fiberglass
High shear and high peel adhesive resists cold flow and oozing
Adhesive bonds well to base film substrates
1 mil ultra smooth clear polyester release liner ensures optimum clarity
