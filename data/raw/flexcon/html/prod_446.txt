FLX000446
TamperMark™ PM 200 White Void II TC-249 V-344 Spec 50K/Q-8
Topcoated thermal transfer printable, tamper evident glossy white small void polyester film coated with a permanent pressure sensitive acrylic adhesive and backed with a kraft release liner
Request information
Home
Technical Data and Details
Availability
Benefits
UL recognized under UL 969 - UL File No. PGGU2.MH10170 Marking and Labeling System Materials – Component
Click to view PDF
Contact: +1 508 885 8200
Features
2.0 mil tamper-evident white polyester film leaves behind "VOID" pattern
Topcoat optimizes ink receptivity with most solvent and UV ink systems (please test for ink adhesion on your specific ink chemistry)
Permanent acrylic pressure-sensitive adhesive bonds well to low- and high-surface energy plastics, painted metal, powder-coated paint, moderately rough surfaces.  polycarbonate and fiberglass
High-peel permanent acrylic adhesive resists cold flow and ooze
Performance, aggressive, permanent pressure-sensitive adhesive bonds well to a variety of surfaces
Backed with a 50 lb. semi-bleached kraft release liner that is Quilon®* coated
Additional Details
Ensure brand security with Flexcon® TamperMark™
tamper proof label materials
.
*Quilon® is a registered trademark of Zaclon, LLC.
