Technical Data and Details
Availability
Click to view PDF
Contact: +1 508 885 8200
Features
5.0 mil gloss topcoated bright brushed silver polyester film offers good durability
Topcoat resists smudging and abrasion when printed with resin and wax/resin thermal transfer ribbons
Permanent acrylic adhesive offers medium tack, high shear and excellent adhesion to a variety of metal and plastic surfaces, including polypropylene, polyethylene, PORON® and Kushon® cellular rubber products
Backed with a 50lb. semi-bleached kraft release liner that is Quilon®* coated to prevent label pickoff†
Additional Details
*PORON® is a registered trademark of Rogers Corporation. Kushon® is a registered trademark of Griswold Products, LLC.
†Quilon® is a registered trademark of Zaclon LLC.
