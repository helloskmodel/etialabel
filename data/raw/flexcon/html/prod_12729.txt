Contact: +1 508 885 8200
Features
2.0 mil transfer adhesive
High-performance adhesive that will permanently adhere to a variety of surfaces such as low-surface energy plastics, painted metal, powder coated paint, polycarbonate and fiberglass
Adheres to smooth and textured TPO’s and other low-energy surfaces.
Backed with an 84 lb. natural kraft polycoated layflat differential release liner
Heavy release liner has excellent moisture stability and is a good base for flatbed and steel rule diecutting
