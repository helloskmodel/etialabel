Flame-Retardant Adhesive Tapes for the Transportation Industry

UL-recognized Flexcon® FlexMount® L-59FR and Flexcon® FlexMark® L-59FR portfolios feature self-extinguishing coated tapes and coated films for a variety of mounting & bonding applications.

Flammability Performance
UL-recognized
FlexMark® and FlexMount® L-59FR products coated at 2.0 mil to 4.0 mil adhesive thickness are rated UL 94 V-0 when coated on 2.0 mil polyimide and 2.0 mil aluminum foil.*
Meets Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards
FlexMark® L-59FR products (2.0 mil polyimide & 2.0 mil foil) at a 2.0 mil adhesive thickness meet industry flame retardancy specifications put forth by the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (FMVSS 302).

Automotive Applications
Meets federal motor vehicle safety standards
FlexMark® L-59FR products (2.0 mil polyimide & 2.0 mil foil) at a 2.0 mil adhesive thickness meet industry flame retardancy specifications put forth by the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (FMVSS 302).
Products that pass the Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) are suitable as component materials in the following automotive applications:
Seat cushions
Seat belts
Headlining
Convertible tops
Armrests
Trim panels
Compartment shelves
Head restraints
Sun visors
Shades
Wheel housing covers
Engine compartment covers
Floor coverings
Aerospace Applications
Complies with federal aviation requirements
FlexMark® L-59FR and FlexMount® L-59FR products at a 2.0 mil adhesive thickness on 2.0 mil polyimide or 2.0 mil aluminum foil pass the following aviation standards:
Vertical 60 sec burn: FAR 25.853 (a) Appendix F, Part I, (a), 1, (i)
Vertical 12 sec burn: FAR 25.853 (a) Appendix F, Part I, (a), 1, (ii)
Heat Release: FAR 25.853 (d) Appendix F, Part IV
Smoke Density: FAR 25.853 (d) Appendix F, Part V
Toxicity Test: BSS 7239
Products that pass the Federal Aviation Requirements (FAR) are suitable as component materials in the following aerospace applications:
Floor coverings
Textiles (draperies & upholstery)
Seat cushions
Floor padding
Trays
Electrical conduits
Air ducting
Cargo covers
Partitions
Structural flooring
Interior wall panels
Gallery structure
Interior ceiling panels
Stowage compartments
Large cabinet walls
