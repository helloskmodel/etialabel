Flexcon® SA Series Silicone Adhesive High-Temperature Bonding & Labeling
Silicone-based adhesives are appropriate for a multitude of applications. Certain types can tolerate sustained temperature extremes from -300°F to 500°F. Some can even go as high as 800°F to 1000°F for limited periods. Available as single- and double-coated tapes, transfer adhesives, and as full label constructions, there is a SA Series adhesive that’s right for the job.

Siliconized Surface Labeling
Silicone adhesives are ideal for long-lasting industrial use. They bond to metals, glass, plastics, and elastomers, providing reliable performance in demanding industrial environments.
Common applications for silicone based adhesives include electronics assembly, automotive components, aerospace systems, medical devices, and industrial gasketing.
Silicone based adhesives can handle high heat while protecting
delicate components
during assembly.
High-Temp Masking
Some manufacturing processes are hot. Silicone-based masking products can handle high heat while protecting delicate components during assembly.
Screen Protection
In electronics, they bond, seal, and protect sensitive parts. They offer great electrical insulation. They also guard against moisture, vibration, and temperature changes. Single-sided silicone tapes make excellent low-tack protection films for electronic screens. Flexcon® SA1000 Series products offer superior adhesion and wet out for ultra clear application to smooth surfaces, while providing consistent, clean, and quiet removability.
Assembly
High-tack silicone adhesives bond quickly to silicone foam & rubber as well as low surface energy plastics, making them an ideal choice for component assembly.
Sound & Vibration Damping
Silicone adhesives create a flexible layer that helps reduce sound and vibration. Manufacturers use them for sound and vibration damping in brake pads, hard drives and motor mounts that can see temperatures as high as 800°F to 1000°F.
