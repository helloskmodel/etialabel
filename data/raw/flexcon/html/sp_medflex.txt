Introducing UL-Verified Flexcon® MedFlex® Plus
Request information
Download brochure
Producing compliant medical device labels has never been easier!
MedFlex® Plus
meets the challenging demands for labeling Class I and II medical devices used outside the body. UL has tested all products for sticking to different surfaces. They also meet IEC 60601-1 3rd edition standards for marking durability. This includes tests with six new cleaning agents based on updated cleaning methods in medical settings.

Easy and FREE UL Adoptions
MedFlex® Plus printable products are immediately adoptable into your UL listing, providing the label stock, surfaces, ribbon, and ink combinations you need to produce compliant medical device labels.
Even better,
Flexcon will credit
the basic adoption fee upon meeting a predetermined purchase threshold.
Color and Finish Options for Every Application
Available in 2 mil clear, white, and silver matte polyester with either a gloss or matte topcoat.

No problem! UL-recognized MedFlex® Overlam products will ensure long-term label legibility in both human readable and bar code scannable labels for the lifetime of the medical device.
