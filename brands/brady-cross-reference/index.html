Authorized brands we represent

Genuine, brand-authorized materials — never grey-market substitutes. This is the core of a reliable label: the right engineered stock, sourced direct from the manufacturer.

Polyonics  USA

Ultra-thin polyimide and PET for PCB reflow, ESD, flame-retardant and laser-marking electronics labels. Sole agent — 20 years.

YS Tech  Japan

Metal, aluminum-foil and inorganic ceramic tags for 300–1300 °C metallurgy, heat-treatment and kilns. Sole agent, Asia-Pacific.

FlexCon  USA

Pressure-sensitive films, adhesives and specialty face-stocks — the material base for demanding converted labels.

Computype  USA

Barcode, traceability and identification systems for regulated, high-mix production environments.

ETIA  Own brand

Full-range PET, PP and specialty converter stock — cryogenic, medical, cable and outdoor — cut in our own die-cutting & slitting factory.
