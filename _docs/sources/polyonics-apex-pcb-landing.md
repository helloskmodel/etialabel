# Polyonics Apex Series — PCB product-landing content (authoritative)

Headline: Introducing the NEW Apex Series – Ultra-Performance Polyimide Label Materials for PCBs
Sub: Next-gen coating for modern PCB lines. Proven gains in flux and cleaner resistance with
excellent print quality and adhesion at high temperatures.

## Value pillars
- Improved Heat Resistance — engineered for extreme thermal cycles; labels stay secure and readable.
- Improved Chemical & Flux Resistance — withstands high-pressure washes and aggressive fluxes.
- Precision Readability — maintains small-font and barcode readability through reflow and cleaning.
- Superior Print Performance — optimized for crisp, high-contrast TTR printability.
- Made in the USA, trusted global supplier.

## Why Apex for PCB manufacturing
- Built for today's fluxes, cleaners and multi-cycle reflow.
- 72% improvement in flux solder performance vs. legacy line (validated in testing).
- 15% improvement in cleaner resistance vs. legacy line (survives high-pressure washes).
- Enhanced ink transfer for more defined images even at small font sizes; extends printhead life;
  maintains readability through highly caustic solutions.
- Coating + adhesive system engineered for elevated temperatures.

## Apex Series SKUs (all REACH/RoHS compliant; ANSI/ESD S20.20 options available)
- XF-101M / XF-102MM — Matte white polyimide, 1 mil / 2 mil, acrylic adhesive. PCB-ready durability + printability.
- XF-101S / XF-102S — Semi-gloss white polyimide, 1 mil / 2 mil, acrylic. Balanced readability + handling.
- XF-101G / XF-102G — Gloss white polyimide, 1 mil / 2 mil, acrylic. Highest brightness for tiny fonts + dense barcodes.
- XF-101SE / XF-102SE / XF-101ME — ESD-safe semi-gloss variants; antistatic construction for ESD-sensitive workflows.

## Thermal exposure guide (typical; see TDS for exact limits)
- Up to 100 hours @ 302°F (150°C)
- 5 minutes @ 500°F (260°C)
- 90 seconds @ 572°F (300°C)

## Applications
PCB identification & WIP labeling; electronic component tracking; asset & warranty labeling.
Compatible with common PCB process chemistries and fluxes. Industries: aerospace, automotive,
electronics, laboratory.

## Print performance
Thermal Transfer (TTR) printability that holds through reflow — crisp high-contrast images;
protects small type + dense barcodes after heat and wash cycles.
