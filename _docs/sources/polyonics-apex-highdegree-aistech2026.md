# Polyonics — HIGHdegree & Apex Series (AISTech 2026 press release)

Source: Polyonics, Inc. press release (AISTech 2026, Booth #219, Pittsburgh PA, May 4–6 2026).
Use for: POLYONICS APEX product-series landing page + HIGHdegree (steel/metals sector) line.

## Apex Series — next-generation high-performance label materials
Positioning: engineered label materials for operations spanning **metals processing and
electronics manufacturing**. Target buyers: automotive suppliers, aerospace tier-ones,
EV battery producers — labels that perform across both worlds.

Key performance specifications:
- **+72%** improved flux solder resistance over previous-generation materials
- **+15%** improved resistance to post-solder cleaning agents (saponifiers, aqueous cleaners, IPA-based)
- Proven heat resistance up to **572°F (300°C)** without charring, yellowing, or print degradation
- **ESD-safe** constructions meeting critical surface resistivity thresholds for electrostatic-sensitive environments
- **UL recognition** supporting compliance labeling across multiple industry standards

## HIGHdegree — track-and-trace labels for steel & aluminum mills
Positioning: proprietary label materials for metal identification in the harshest mill
environments (steel + aluminum producers). Addresses extreme process temps, aggressive
chemical exposure, elimination of manual marking.

Key performance specifications:
- Adhesion + barcode readability maintained through **hot-rolling, annealing, and reheat furnace** processes
- Proprietary coatings engineered to resist **molten metal splatter** that destroys conventional tags
- Designed for **pickling lines, cleaning baths, and harsh mill chemistry**
- Machine-scannable barcodes — positive identification **from slab to shipment**
- **Direct thermal and thermal transfer** print compatibility for integration with existing MES systems
Applications named: slab tracking, coil identification, heat-resistant adhesion, chemical resistance.
